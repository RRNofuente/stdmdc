ABOUT US

About St. Thomas Diagnostic Medical and Dental Clinic, Inc.

St. Thomas Diagnostic Medical and Dental Clinic has been a trusted name in healthcare since its establishment in 1992. Specializing in comprehensive health screening for overseas employment applicants, we take pride in offering timely and accurate medical assessments to ensure the well-being of Filipino workers embarking on international careers.

Quality, Efficiency, and Expertise

Our clinic thrives on providing quick and reliable results, understanding the importance of time for both job seekers and employers. We are fully accredited by the government and certified under ISO 9001 and SGS standards, which guarantees that our services meet the highest industry benchmarks.

State-of-the-Art Facilities and Skilled Professionals

With over two decades of experience, we have continuously enhanced our facilities and services to cater to the growing demands of pre-employment medical examinations. Our team of licensed and experienced medical staff operates advanced diagnostic equipment, ensuring that every test and result is precise and dependable. We adhere strictly to DOH-POE-MARINA standards, offering medical services that consistently exceed expectations.

Mission

To be committed in providing high quality medical health services to all our clients and patients of whom we believe deserves the best medical health care.

Vision

To be in the forefront of medical health screening and evaluation through professional skill and reliable services utilizing modern medical facilities at par with the international standards.

Goals

To establish the clinic as a key player in the medical industry by building its reputation and exceeding in providing quality health services to clients and patients.
