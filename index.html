ABOUT US

St. Thomas Diagnostic Medical and Dental Clinic has been a trusted provider of reliable, compassionate, and client-focused healthcare services since 1992. We specialize in delivering comprehensive health screenings and evaluations for overseas employment applicants.

Our commitment to delivering accurate results promptly reflects our understanding of the value of time for both applicants and employers. Accredited by the government and certified under the ISO 9001 Quality Management System and SGS standards, we are well-equipped to meet your healthcare screening needs.
